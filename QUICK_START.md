# 🚀 Battal Pro Max - Quick Start Guide

## Sign In / Sign Up - The 3 Roles

### 1️⃣ **Job Seeker** 👤
The person looking for a job
- Browse job opportunities
- Swipe like/pass on jobs (Tinder-style)
- Build profile with experience & skills
- Earn achievement badges
- Get personalized recommendations

### 2️⃣ **Company Admin** 🏢
The company representative managing recruitment
- Post job openings
- Review applicants
- See who applied for jobs
- Manage company profile
- View hiring analytics

### 3️⃣ **Recruiter** 👥
The recruiting professional
- Post jobs for companies
- Screen candidates
- Advanced filtering
- Manage multiple positions
- Match candidates with skills

## ✍️ Creating Your Own Account

### Step 1: Go to Sign Up
Click "Create Account" tab on login page

### Step 2: Select Your Role
Choose from 3 options:
- **Job Seeker** (👤 icon)
- **Company** (🏢 icon)
- **Recruiter** (👥 icon)

### Step 3: Fill in Details
- First Name
- Last Name
- Email
- Password (min 6 characters)
- Confirm Password

### Step 4: Click Create
Your account is ready!

## 🔧 Troubleshooting Tips

### Didn't See Login Page?
→ Already logged in? Check the role switcher in top right

### Want to Switch Roles?
→ Click your role name in top right, choose different role

### Can't Create Account?
→ Password must be at least 6 characters
→ All fields are required

### Connection Issues?
→ Make sure backend PHP server is running
→ Check database connection in backend
